Frequently asked questions

Or get in touch via the form and let us know your questions, ideas or suggestions. We'd love to hear from you!

When will The Time Key be available?

If there are no major setbacks with our prototype, The Time Key will be available to pre-order via Kickstarter from the 2nd October 2016.

What is Kickstarter?

Kickstarter is a really great crowd-funding tool which allows people to help bring new products, like ours, to life. By being a "backer", you play a vital role in the development of the product you are supporting. Your donation will be rewarded with a gift which could be your very own The Time Key money box, once they have been manufactured.

How much can I donate to Kickstarter?

This all depends on what you want to receive back as a reward. You can donate as little as £1 to receive a personal thank you message from all of The Time Key team or you can increase your donation to receive one of the very first The Time Key money boxes off the production line. The donation amount has yet to be decided for the money boxes themselves as we are still in the design process, but be sure to check out our social media pages as the first 50 backers can take advantage of the super-early-bird special of 25% off the RRP.

How long after Kickstarter will I receive my The Time Key?

If we are successful with our Kickstarter campaign, we are looking to ship the product to all of our backers around 6 months after the campaign has ended (by April 2016).

How does The Time Key work?

Simply choose the desired amount of days you wish to save for, put the lid back onto the base, press the set button and the box will lock until the timer reaches zero.

How long can I lock The Time Key money box for?

365 days on one set of new batteries.

Is there an override to access the money inside?

Absolutely not! We have removed all temptation to increase the users’ chances of reaching their desired saving goal.

How much can The Time Key money box hold?

Using our clever 3D CAD software, we have calculated that the box can hold 500 £2 coins. However, the box can hold a lot more in notes.

What colour boxes are available?

Currently we have the following colour combinations:
Red lid/red base, Black lid/black base, White lid/white base, Green lid/clear base, Pink lid/clear base and Blue lid/clear base.

How does the mobile app work?

From the moment you lock the box, you can start the countdown timer on your phone to stay up to date with how long is remaining until your set saving goal. Every time you enter a unit of money into your money box, you can manually enter this amount into the app. Additionally, you can set up notifications to remind you that you need to enter ‘X’ amount of money per day, week or month to ensure you are on track to reach your target.
