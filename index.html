A NEW WAY TO SAVE MONEY

Time after time, we set our savings goals but end up falling short due to dipping into those savings to cover other exenses. Well now that is a thing of the past, with our new innovation you can save with the piece of mind that your saved money is locked away from your self to ensure you reach your saving goal.

HOW IT WORKS

Built with a time delay locking mechanisn that won't give up if you do.

  • Choose an amount of days between 1 and 365.
  • Once the days are set the box will lock, go to sleep and remain locked until your day arrives.
  • Remaining time can be checked at any time by pressing the control button to wake The Time Key.
