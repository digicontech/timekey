A NEW WAY TO SAVE MONEY

Time after time, we set savings goals but end up falling short due to dipping into those savings to cover other expenses. Well, now that is a thing of the past. With our new innovation, you can save money with peace of mind that your savings are locked away to ensure you reach your goal.

HOW IT WORKS

Built with a time-delay mechanism, it will not give up if you do.

  1. Choose a number of days between 1 and 365
  2. Once the day is set, the box will lock, go to sleep and remain locked until your day arrives.
  3. The remaining time can be checked at any time by pressing the control button to wake The Time Key.
  4. Once the countdown reaches zero, you will receive a notification via the app to let you know the box is ready to be unlocked.

The only thing left to do is fill it but, unfortunately, we cannot help with that one, that is down to you!

Perfect Saving Aid for Children

Teaching children the importance of saving is vital. The Time Key will restrict access to accumulated funds to help your child reach their saving goals.

Backed By Research

Research has proven that a level of willpower is required to meet saving goals. Inserting allocated funds into The Time Key will decrease the amount of willpower required as the savings will not be accessible.

Deeper Than your Pockets

We have calculated that The Time Key can hold in excess of five-hundred £1 coins. However, it can store a whole more in notes to help you save big.

Simple Set Up

With only three control buttons, The Time Key is simple to set-up. Be sure to download our app to countdown your remaining days.

Superior Design

Designed to complement any home, The Time Key would not look out of place in any room (perhaps with the exception of the bathroom, but that is your choice).

Energy Efficient

As it is powered by two AA batteries that will last at least twelve months, we have calculated that the user can wake The Time Key approximately 9,000 times.

We have developed an App to compliment The Time Key. This will be included, free of charge, with every Time Key moneybox that is purchased.

Once you have pressed the set button on TTK, you can also set your timeframe within the App. You will then be able to monitor the time remaining until TTK opens, wherever you are, at any time. Additionally, you can set the date of an event that you are saving for, giving you a countdown until, for example, you go away on holiday.

Also, most importantly, you will be able to track how much money you have saved within TTK. Whenever you add money to TTK, you can simply open the App and add the amount to your total. Therefore, this will help you to reach your saving goal in an easy-to-read format.

With push-notifications to alert you daily, weekly or monthly with a suggested amount to insert into your moneybox to reach your target, the App is everything you need to assist you in reaching your savings goal.
